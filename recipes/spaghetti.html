Spaghetti

Description

A simple but delicious traditional Italian meal

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Grated cheese (optional)
  • Spices (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add noodles
  3. Heat sauce
  4. Wait 10 minutes
  5. Drain water
  6. Add sauce
  7. Optional: add cheese or spices
  8. Serve
