Hamburger

Description

A cookout tradition!

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Bun
  • Slice of cheese (optional)
  • Condiments (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat patty on grill or stove
  2. Flip periodically to cook both sides evenly
  3. Remove when cooked desired amount (medium, well-done)
  4. Check inside with a fork or knife
  5. Cook a little more as needed
  6. Remove from grill or stove and place on bun
  7. Optional: add cheese or condiments
  8. Serve
