Baked Potato

Description

Delicious and loaded with vitamins!

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Butter
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Chives (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Place potato in oven
  3. Wait 10 minutes
  4. Check with a fork - is it soft?
  5. If not wait a couple more minutes
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 as needed
  7. Remove potato from oven
  8. Cut open potato and add butter
  9. Optional: add sour cream or chives
  10. Serve
